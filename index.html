Why use AsUBus?



Features and Overview


Synopsis

Have an iPhone? Live in Boone, NC? Stop relying on AppalCART's confusing schedule and use AsUBus's streamlined interface instead! With a full range of features, AsUBus presents all of the same info as the AppleCART schedule, but in a much less confusing and frustrating way. With a simple layout, AsUBus gives you the information you need on the go. Also it is completely free! Get it on the app store today!


History

Brian Clee began developing AsUBus in the summer between his Freshman and Sophomore years at Appalachian State University. Brian developed the app out of frustration when using AppalCART's website on a mobile device. AsUBus launched September 6, 2012 after just 3 months of development and has been growing in use and popularity ever since. Within the first two weeks there were over 1000 users, and as of January 1st, 2014 there were 8646 users. Brian is still the developer of AsUBus and will keep it live and FREE as long as possible for App State students and locals.


Coverage

AsUBus displays routes by the seasons they run, and gives the user an interactive way of finding the stop they want. AsUBus contains the complete up to date AppalCART schedule and always has data on the latest route changes and additions.


Awesome Support

With an integrated report system, users can report any bus closures and difficulties they come across while riding the AppalCART. As well, the developer behind AsUBus is always looking for new ways to make the app better and serve the needs of all users around Boone, NC.


Responsive Design

With the ability to search for stops on an interactive map, users are able to see exactly where they are in Boone and how close the nearest stop is. As well, once on the stop detail page, users will see a constantly refreshing countdown of when the next bus should arrive.


Favorites and News

Frequent users of AsUBus may find it tiring to navigate through the map to get to their most used stops over and over again. This is why a favorites feature exists that allows users to save all of their most used stops. Furthermore, AsUBus is linked to the AppalCART official twitter, giving users up to date news on any stop closings.

Disclaimer

This application presents stop information based on the official schedule posted by AppalCart. AsUBus in no way tracks the busses to present real time data, it simply displays the scheduled time estimations. AsUBus is in no way affiliated with AppalCART or Appalachian State University.
